How to share a movie from Google Drive

  1. Upload your movie file to Google Drive
  2. Right-click the file → Share → Anyone with the link → Viewer
  3. Click Copy link — it looks like: drive.google.com/file/d/FILE_ID/view
  4. Paste it below and click Embed Movie

💡 Both of you can use the Drive player controls — just communicate over call to stay in sync ♥

Screen Share — Step by Step

📞
Step 1 — Start a video call

Open WhatsApp, FaceTime, Google Meet, or any video call with your partner

🖥
Step 2 — Share your screen

Tap the Share Screen button in your call app. On WhatsApp: ⋮ → Share screen

🎬
Step 3 — Play your movie

Open Netflix, Prime Video, your local player, or any streaming service and hit play

🔊
Tip — Share audio too

On desktop: check "Share audio" when screen sharing so your partner hears the movie

Best apps for screen sharing a movie:
📱 WhatsApp — tap ⋮ → Share screen (mobile & desktop)
💻 Google Meet — great for desktop, share a Chrome tab with audio
🍎 FaceTime (iOS 15+) — SharePlay streams video apps natively
🎮 Discord — Go Live supports screen share with audio on desktop
